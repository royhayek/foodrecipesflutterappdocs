Firebase Push Notifications

Why I need it

You will need to send notifications to user devices.

In order to make our push notification work we have to follow the steps below:

  1. Go to your firebase console and press on Project Settings

  1. Now in your Project Settings press on Cloud Messaging and copy your Server Key

  1. Go to your admin panel and login using the following credentials:
  • U: admin@example.com
  • P: admin

Click on the avatar, and go to your profile. Change both username and password.

  1. Go to Settings and paste your server key in the Firebase FCM key text field then press Save at the bottom of the page
